Over the past year,

I’ve been building my journey in frontend development — a field I entered almost by chance, but quickly grew to love and found myself deeply connected to. What started as simple curiosity soon turned into passion, motivating me to explore and enjoy every step of this craft.

During this time, I have worked on websites, experimental projects, and freelance tasks that focus on writing clean, efficient code and delivering smooth user experiences. I also care deeply about elegant design, ensuring that my projects not only perform well but also look refined and professional.

I’ve gained hands-on experience with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, while also exploring modern tools and frameworks such as React, along with using Git and GitHub for version control. These skills have helped me turn ideas and designs into responsive, interactive interfaces that work beautifully across all devices.

I pay special attention to the small details that make a big difference in user experience — whether it’s aligning elements precisely, ensuring cross-device compatibility, or adding subtle touches that make interaction more enjoyable.

Although my professional experience is less than a year, my passion for continuous learning and growth keeps pushing me forward. I regularly work on personal projects, stay up to date with the latest trends in web development, and engage with developer communities to exchange knowledge.

Looking ahead, I aim to deepen my expertise in React and Next.js, collaborate with innovative teams, and contribute to building impactful web applications. My ultimate goal is to merge technical precision with elegant design to deliver solutions that are both functional and visually compelling.
